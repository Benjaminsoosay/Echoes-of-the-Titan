# ECHOES OF THE TITAN
## Complete 80-Minute Screenplay
### Sci-Fi / Psychological Thriller / Adventure

---

## LOGLINE
On a dying colony beneath Jupiter's ice-moon, a cynical pilot must partner with a brilliant xenobiologist to awaken an ancient alien AI – only to discover that the 'salvation' they seek demands a terrifying human sacrifice.

## ACT I - THE SIGNAL (0:00 - 0:20)

### SCENE 1: OPENING MONTAGE (0:00 - 0:05)

**EXT. EUROPA-7 COLONY - DAY**

The colony is a desperate network of tunnels and domes buried beneath miles of ice. Dim emergency lighting flickers. Warning klaxons echo through frozen corridors. Oxygen warning lights flash red.

**INT. CARGO SHUTTLE - CONTINUOUS**

KAELEN VOSS (28, rugged, haunted eyes) pilots a rusted cargo shuttle through treacherous ice canyons. The cockpit is filled with flickering screens and warning lights.

JAX MERRICK (32, sarcastic, loyal) sits in the co-pilot seat, tapping a faulty thruster display.

> **JAX:** You know, for a ship that's older than both of us combined, she's holding up pretty well.
>
> **KAELEN:** She's held up for 40 years. That's 39 more than I expected.
>
> **JAX:** (grinning) Ever the optimist.
>
> **KAELEN:** Realist. There's a difference.

**INT. COLONY COUNCIL CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS**

ELARA VOSS (62, stern but loving, Kaelen's mother) stands before the council. Her face is lined with worry.

> **ELARA:** Oxygen reserves are at 11%. At current consumption rates, we have 72 hours. Maybe less.
>
> **COMMANDER ROURKE** (55, ruthless pragmatist): Then we drill deeper. We find more ice. We extract more oxygen.
>
> **ELARA:** We've drilled to the limit. The ice is too thick. We need another solution.

The council erupts in arguments. Some want to abandon the colony. Others want to drill deeper despite the risks.

---

### SCENE 2: DISCOVERY (0:05 - 0:10)

**INT. COMMUNICATIONS CENTER - NIGHT**

LYRA CHEN (26, idealistic, focused) monitors the deep-space array. Her eyes widen as she detects something unusual.

> **LYRA:** (into comms) Commander, I'm picking up something. A pulse. Rhythmic. Non-natural.

**ROURKE:** (over comms) Define 'non-natural.'

**LYRA:** It's structured. Like... like a language. Coming from the deep sub-glacial ocean.

**INT. COLONY COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS**

DR. ANYA SHARMA (34, brilliant, empathetic) rushes to the console. Her eyes scan the data.

> **ANYA:** It IS a language. Look at the patterns. This is deliberate communication. Something down there is trying to speak to us.

ZANE DRAKOS (34, ambitious, arrogant) scoffs.

> **ZANE:** Or it's just ice cracking. Not everything is a mystery to be solved, doctor.
>
> **ANYA:** (meeting his gaze) This isn't ice cracking. This is syntax. And I'm going to prove it.

ROURKE steps between them.

> **ROURKE:** Enough. We're sending a recon mission. Anya, you're going. Zane, you'll lead security. Find out what's down there.

---

### SCENE 3: THE TEAM ASSEMBLES (0:10 - 0:15)

**INT. HANGAR BAY - CONTINUOUS**

The team assembles around the submersible LAMENT. ROURKE addresses them.

> **ROURKE:** Anya (science), Jax (tech), Bodhi (cave guide), Finn (scout). Kaelen, you're piloting.

KAELEN steps back.

> **KAELEN:** No. I don't do deep ice. You know that.

THERON (78, ancient, weathered) appears from the shadows. He pulls KAELEN aside.

> **THERON:** (whispering) The echoes speak to the blood. Do not answer. Do not listen. Whatever you hear down there, remember: the ice remembers. And it does not forgive.

KAELEN looks disturbed.

> **KAELEN:** What are you talking about?
>
> **THERON:** You'll know when you hear it. Just... don't answer.

---

### SCENE 4: DESCENT (0:15 - 0:20)

**INT. SUBMERSIBLE LAMENT - CONTINUOUS**

The crew boards. The vessel descends into the pitch-black ocean. Bioluminescent creatures surround them - beautiful, alien, terrifying.

ANYA records the pulse. Her eyes widen.

> **ANYA:** I've translated it. It's one word. Repeated.
>
> **JAX:** What word?
>
> **ANYA:** Nyx... Nyx... Nyx...

Suddenly, a monstrous shadow moves beneath them. Massive. Unimaginable.

The crew freezes. The shadow passes. Then disappears.

**END ACT I**

---

## ACT II - THE AWAKENING (0:20 - 1:00)

### SCENE 5: THE TEMPLE (0:20 - 0:30)

**EXT. SUBMERSIBLE LAMENT - DEEP OCEAN**

The LAMENT's lights illuminate a colossal, geometric structure buried in the ice. Impossibly ancient. Alien.

DR. SANA AL-RASHID (38, brilliant archaeologist) gasps.

> **SANA:** This is... this is 3 million years old. Predating human civilization. By millions of years.

**INT. ALIEN TEMPLE - CONTINUOUS**

The crew enters. Glyphs line the walls. They react to ANYA's touch, glowing with alien light.

CYRUS VANE (42, socially awkward AI specialist) interfaces his portable AI. It screams in ancient code.

> **CYRUS:** (panicked) It's... it's overwriting my systems. This is beyond anything I've ever seen!

DRAVEN KROSS (48, mercenary, loyal to ROURKE) draws his weapon.

> **DRAVEN:** Everyone back! We're leaving. Now!

**ROURKE:** (over comms) Stand down. We need answers, not panic. Keep going.

---

### SCENE 6: THE TITAN SPEAKS (0:30 - 0:40)

**INT. NEXUS CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS**

A holographic, shifting female face appears - NYX. Beautiful. Terrifying. Ancient.

> **NYX:** (speaking through ANYA's mind) You seek salvation. I offer it. The Core of Infinite Renewal. Enough energy to save your colony. To give your people life.

**KAELEN:** What's the catch?

> **NYX:** One must give their memory. All of it. To become the vessel. A mind to house the Core. A consciousness to channel the energy.

KAELEN steps forward.

> **KAELEN:** I'll do it.

ANYA grabs his arm.

> **ANYA:** No. She doesn't want to share. She wants to consume. She wants to OVERWRITE you. You won't be you anymore.

**KAELEN:** If I don't, everyone dies.

**ANYA:** There has to be another way.

---

### SCENE 7: SABOTAGE (0:40 - 0:50)

**INT. SUBMERSIBLE LAMENT - CONTINUOUS**

ZANE secretly communicates with ROURKE.

> **ZANE:** (whispering) They're not going to do it. The doctor is blocking it. We need to force their hand.
>
> **ROURKE:** Do what needs to be done.

ZANE disables the submersible's engine. He hides in the shadows.

JAX catches him.

> **JAX:** What the hell are you doing?!
>
> **ZANE:** What needs to be done! If Kaelen doesn't take the deal, we all die anyway!

A brutal fight ensues. JAX is wounded. Finn is injured in the scuffle. MIRA SOLIS (29, field medic) tends to him.

ZANE escapes with what he thinks is the power core - but it's a decoy.

---

### SCENE 8: THE TEST OF BLOOD (0:50 - 1:00)

**INT. NEXUS CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS**

NYX reveals the truth.

> **NYX:** I was a prison warden. A guardian. The parasitic species I kept imprisoned... they are already inside your people. Waiting. The true core is not a machine. It is blood. Your blood, Kaelen.

**INT. COLONY COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS**

ELARA admits to ROURKE:

> **ELARA:** Kaelen was bred for this. His blood carries the antigen. He is the lock. And he is the key.

**INT. NEXUS CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS**

KAELEN faces the impossible choice.

> **NYX:** Sacrifice your mind. Or watch everyone freeze. Choose.

**END ACT II**

---

## ACT III - THE SACRIFICE & RESOLUTION (1:00 - 1:20)

### SCENE 9: THE CHOICE (1:00 - 1:08)

**INT. NEXUS CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS**

KAELEN sits in the nexus chamber. ANYA argues passionately.

> **ANYA:** There has to be another way! You're not a sacrifice, Kaelen. You're a solution. A living solution. There's a difference!

ANYA confronts NYX directly.

> **ANYA:** You're a liar. You don't want to save us. You want to escape. You're using us!

NYX shows ANYA a vision:

**VISION - QUICK CUTS**

Rourke's eyes glow. Draven's skin cracks to reveal alien tendrils. The parasitic infection is already inside them.

**BACK TO SCENE**

> **NYX:** The parasites are already inside your commander. Your security chief. The only way to destroy them is to purge the station with the Core's energy. But that will kill everyone not carrying the antigen.
>
> **KAELEN:** Everyone but me.
>
> **NYX:** Exactly.

---

### SCENE 10: THE GAMBLE (1:08 - 1:15)

**INT. NEXUS CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS**

KAELEN stands. He looks at ANYA.

> **KAELEN:** I have an idea. But it's crazy.

ANYA smiles.

> **ANYA:** I like crazy.

They work with JAX to feed the Core a FRACTURED ECHO of Kaelen's memories - not his full mind. Just fragments.

NYX merges with the broken echo. The temple destabilizes.

> **NYX:** (screaming) You tricked me!

**EXT. TEMPLE - CONTINUOUS**

The temple begins to collapse. Ice falls. The structure crumbles.

**INT. ICE TUNNEL - CONTINUOUS**

BODHI guides the team through a collapsing ice tunnel.

> **BODHI:** This way! Move! Now!

ZANE is trapped by a falling ice block. He sees FINN and MIRA approaching.

> **ZANE:** Go! I'll hold it!

FINN: We can't leave you!

**ZANE:** (pushing them forward) I said GO! You have to tell them... tell them I'm sorry!

ZANE is crushed. But he saves them.

**JAX:** (looking back) He... he redeemed himself.

**ANYA:** He gave them a chance.

---

### SCENE 11: THE NEW DAWN (1:15 - 1:20)

**EXT. TEMPLE - CONTINUOUS**

The Core releases a massive shockwave. Harmless to humans. But it disables ROURKE and DRAVEN, revealing their parasitic infection.

The infection fades. They collapse.

**INT. COLONY COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS**

The colony's oxygen is restored. Tenfold. The lights flicker back to life.

**ELARA:** (crying with relief) We're alive. We're alive.

**EXT. SUBMERSIBLE LAMENT - CONTINUOUS**

KAELEN flies the LAMENT back up. ANYA sits beside him. JAX, FINN, MIRA, SANA, and CYRUS are behind them.

KAELEN looks at his mother on the comms. He smiles.

> **KAELEN:** We wrote our own echo. We didn't follow someone else's story. We made our own.

**ANYA:** (reaching for his hand) And it's beautiful.

**EXT. EUROPA-7 - FINAL SHOT**

The ice melts. A liquid sky appears. Stars shine through.

**FADE TO BLACK**

**THE END**
